Charlotte Murray

Charlotte Murray is a senior at Eastern Washington University, where she is majoring in Visual Communication Design and is working on a User Experience Certificate. She has many interests ranging from motion work to UX/UI design, as well as Mixed Reality technologies. Once she graduates, she plans to pursue a job in one of these emergent technologies.

While in school, Charlotte interned withLaboratory Spokane,an art nonprofit, creating promotion videos and motion animations for their upcoming projects. She enjoys bringing the beauty out of everything she works with and hopes to continue to make an impact with the work that she does.

Since Charlotte grew up in a military family and moved around her whole childhood, she has had many different life experiences. She is able to view things from more than one perspective and focuses on bringing that into her designs. When Charlotte isn't working at her computer, she enjoys listening to music and spending time with friends, family and her two cats.
